About

Impulse Pause is a lightweight anti-impulse-buying app: capture a purchase idea, wait, and then decide deliberately.

The exploratory smoke suite validates navigation, console errors, and HTTP failures.

How it works

  1. Add an item with a wait time.
  2. Come back when it is ready to buy.
  3. Choose Bought or Skipped and learn from your pattern.
